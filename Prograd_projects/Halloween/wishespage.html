Happy Halloween

It's 31st October and it's raining pumpkins. And, that means it's Halloweeen. AllHalloWeen, Hallo means holy and ween meaning evening. That's how Halloween was formed. Dressing up as spirits & ghosts is a part of Halloween. It's because people believe that wearing scary costumes would keep the evils spirits away. That's also the reason why people carve pumpkin & keep it outside home to ward off ghosts.
